## Qualification Methodology

Each lead scored on 5 dimensions (1-5 each, max 25):

1. **Property Quality** — Photo quality, design, amenities, guest experience potential
2. **Commercial Viability** — ADR level ($200+/night preferred), review sentiment, occupancy signals
3. **Digital Weakness** — 5 = no website; 4 = terrible website; 3 = mediocre; 2 = decent; 1 = good (skip)
4. **Independence** — 5 = clearly owner-operated; 3 = small local manager; 1 = big management company
5. **Approachability** — Contact info available, responsive, active on social

### Priority Tiers

- **Hot (21-25)**: Approach immediately — 24 leads
- **Warm (16-20)**: Approach in wave 2 — 75 leads
- **Cold (<16)**: Skip or revisit later — filtered out

## Digital Weakness Patterns Observed

### Strongest Nestino opportunities (most common digital gaps):
1. **No independent website** — Listed only on OTA platforms or aggregator directories (30+ leads)
2. **Outdated website design** — Non-responsive, slow, poor mobile UX (15+ leads)
3. **No SSL/HTTPS** — Some still on HTTP only (3-5 leads)
4. **Thin content** — Website exists but has only 2-3 pages, no blog/content (20+ leads)
5. **No multilingual presence** — English-only even in markets with diverse source travelers
6. **No schema markup** — Zero structured data for AI/search engines
7. **Generic domain** — Using booking platform URLs or generic domains (e.g., positano.org)
8. **Aggregator-dependent** — Listed on 3-4 aggregators but no owned digital presence

## Recommended Approach Order

### Wave 1: Hot Leads (first 2 weeks)
Start with the 24 Hot leads. Prioritize:
- Properties with NO website at all (pre-build has highest wow factor)
- Properties with very high ADR (higher revenue = stronger ROI pitch)
- Properties in Bali and Greece (largest market opportunity)

### Wave 2: Warm Leads (weeks 3-6)
Work through the 75 Warm leads, prioritizing:
- Properties with outdated/weak websites (clear before/after story)
- Properties with strong reviews but weak online presence
- Independent owner-operators (faster decision cycle)

### Wave 3: Expand Search (ongoing)
- Deep-dive into Instagram hashtag research per destination
- Facebook group monitoring for new villa announcements
- Local real estate portal scanning (sahibinden.com, immobiliare.it, etc.)
- Google Maps systematic sweeps per sub-area
